About us

Nails to Go & Spa offers superior service in a relaxing, comfort environment. . We provide a wide selection of nail enhancements including manicures & pedicures, threading, waxing, eyelash extensions and facials. We listen and effectively respond to your needs and Our friendly, fully trained nail technicians will pamper your hands and feet as you sit surrounded by the sights, sounds and aromas of nature...

At Nails to Go & Spa, our highest priority is satisfied customers. You are important to us and superior customer service is the hallmark of Nails to Go & Spa. We are proud to serve you.
